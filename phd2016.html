HARDWARE VILLAGE
AND
LOCKPICK VILLAGE
ON POSITIVE HACK DAYS 2016

Team of the project decided to take part in the sixth annual forum PHDays.

Fans of hardware will be able to "feel" the equipment and attend master classes, where guys will share the knowledge in the field of low-level programming and hacking.

Hardware Village is designed both for experienced professionals as well as for beginners and enthusiasts. For two days, visitors will be able to attend master classes and lectures of experienced hackers, who will talk about their experiences. For everybody will be available a lot of hacking hardware, on which you can not only to look, but also to test.

The first day will be dedicated to wired networks and data interfaces: Ethernet, 1-Wire, UART, JTAG, SPI, USB, CAN. Hardware Village team will tell you how to choose the equipment for specific tasks and how to work with it right.

You will learn all about multimeters, oscilloscopes, logic analyzers and see some handmade hacker devices based on Arduino, ARM, FPGA. The theme of the second day will be wireless networks: a wide frequency range from 125 kHz to 5 GHz, the popular communication protocols - RFID, NFC, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. Also, the organizers have prepared a master class and competition with the SDR. We are inviting everyone to participate. And do not forget to bring your own devices!
